Current Activities

Hedtek works in a broad variety of IT and IT-related fields.

Our overriding interest is in making complex systems incredibly easy-to-use.

We are currently engaged in the design and development of CNC machines to enable the construction of higher precision CNC machines.

Hedtek's owner is the CTO of Prevayl, an innovative health sciences and wearable technology company.

Past: Mid 2016 – Early 2020

Hedtek was formerly a vehicle for senior-level consultancy and contracting services.

These were coupled with pro bono open source development and startup mentoring.

Past: Mid 2007 – Mid 2016

In its earliest incarnation Hedtek was a Manchester-based software house with strong interests in technical and strategic consultancy.

Besides straightforward commercial work for clients building and running production systems, we performed applied research for several UK government agencies.

One strand of research established the basis for a subsequent and ongoing multi-million pound data analytics programme in the UK's Higher Education sector.

Hedtek developed Gradient; an electronic assessment system for universities and high schools. Gradient primarily focuses on ease-of-use for students and assessors.

We retain our interests in further exploitation of Gradient's patent-protected technology.

After nine years of successful operation, Hedtek was closed as a software house to enable its owner to make a lifestyle-motivated move to London.
